## Why

Students and visitors need a centralized AI assistant to quickly access campus information about hostels, transport, notices, departments, clubs, and facilities without navigating complex systems or calling multiple offices.

## What Changes

Create an AI-powered campus chatbot system that can answer comprehensive queries about Adamas campus operations, services, and facilities.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `campus-chatbot`: AI assistant that answers campus information queries (hostels, transport, notices, departments, clubs, facilities, academic information)

## Impact

- Integrates with existing hostel, transport, notice, club, and department systems
- Provides accessible campus information through natural language interface
- Reduces administrative overhead for routine information requests
- Enhances user experience with instant campus knowledge access
